We're excited to launch the new Pittsburgh Recorder Society website! After outgrowing our old WordPress site, we've built a fresh, modern home on the web for our community of recorder players.

## What You'll Find Here

- **Meeting information** — Everything you need to know about our monthly gatherings, including schedule, location, and what to expect.
- **Events** — Upcoming concerts, workshops, and special events.
- **Resources** — Curated links to free sheet music, recorder organizations, teachers, and more.
- **News** — Announcements and updates from the society.

## Stay Connected

The best way to stay up to date is to join us at a meeting! We meet on the **3rd Sunday of each month** from September through May at First United Methodist Church in Shadyside.

You can also follow us on [Facebook](https://www.facebook.com/PittsburghRecorderSociety) for the latest updates and event announcements.

We look forward to seeing you at a meeting soon!
