The next round of the American Recorder Society's free, virtual Beginners' Course for alto recorder is here just in time for Play-the-Recorder Month!

What better way to celebrate than to take up the recorder — or get a refresher if it's been a while? The course is led by Jennifer Carpenter and is open to anyone.

This is a great opportunity if you've been curious about joining us at a meeting but want to build some confidence first. And of course, our own beginner coaching with Fred Moyes at every monthly meeting is always available too.

For details and registration, visit the [American Recorder Society](https://americanrecorder.org) website.
