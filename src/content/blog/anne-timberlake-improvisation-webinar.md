Recorder player and educator Anne Timberlake is offering a Wednesday Webinar: **"On A Ground: Intro to Improvisation"** on March 11, 2026 at 7:00 PM CT.

Improvisation over a ground bass was a core skill for musicians in the Renaissance and Baroque periods, and it's a wonderful way to deepen your musical understanding. Anne is an excellent teacher who makes these topics accessible for players at all levels.

Anne also writes a [blog](https://annetimberlake.com) with practical advice for recorder players — her latest post offers tips on how to begin on a high note.

Visit [annetimberlake.com](https://annetimberlake.com) for registration and more information.
