Dreaming of spring? The [Amherst Early Music](https://www.amherstearlymusic.org) Spring Break Workshop is coming up in April, and registration is open now.

Amherst workshops are always a highlight of the early music calendar, with top-notch faculty and a welcoming atmosphere for players at all levels. They also offer online classes on weekends throughout the year, including sessions with Anne Timberlake.

Several PRS members have attended Amherst workshops in the past — if you're thinking about going, ask around at our next meeting for firsthand recommendations!

Visit [amherstearlymusic.org](https://www.amherstearlymusic.org) for details and registration.
