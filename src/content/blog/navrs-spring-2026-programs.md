The [North American Virtual Recorder Society](https://americanrecorder.org) (NAVRS) has announced its spring 2026 programs, continuing their season as they celebrate their fifth year as an online recorder community and ARS chapter.

Coming up: **Medieval Mondays** with Annette Bauer on **March 16 at 12:30 PM ET**. These sessions explore medieval music repertoire and are open to all.

NAVRS has been a wonderful resource for recorder players everywhere, offering virtual workshops and playing sessions that complement what local chapters like ours do in person. If you can't always make it to our Sunday meetings in Shadyside, their online programs are a great way to stay connected to the recorder community.
