Registration for the [Mideast Early Music Workshop](https://www.earlymusicweekmideast.org) is now open!

Mideast has been meeting at LaRoche University in Pittsburgh during July for decades. It's primarily for recorder, but there are also classes in viol, baroque flute, voice, early brass, and crumhorn. The instructors are extremely knowledgeable but also approachable — it's a wonderful learning environment.

Many members of our chapter have attended in years past and can answer any questions you might have. Ask around at our next meeting for firsthand recommendations, or check out the website at [earlymusicweekmideast.org](https://www.earlymusicweekmideast.org).

Happy spring — celebrate by playing recorder!
