As we move into the spring portion of our 2025–2026 season, we have some wonderful meetings ahead.

## Upcoming Meetings

Our regular meetings continue on the 3rd Sunday of each month:

- **March 15** — Spring repertoire featuring Renaissance dance music
- **April 19** — Baroque ensemble works
- **May 17** — Season finale with a mix of favorites

All meetings run from 2:00 to 5:00 PM at First United Methodist Church, with beginner coaching available from 2:00 to 3:00 PM.

## Looking Ahead

We're also planning a spring gathering — more details to come. Keep an eye on this space and our [Facebook page](https://www.facebook.com/PittsburghRecorderSociety) for announcements.

Bring your recorders and join us!
