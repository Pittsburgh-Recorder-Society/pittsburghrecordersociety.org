Join us for our annual Spring Play Day! This special event is an extended session with small ensemble coaching and a wider variety of repertoire than our regular monthly meetings.

## Schedule

- **10:00 AM** — Registration and warm-up
- **10:30 AM – 12:30 PM** — Morning session: Small ensemble coaching
- **12:30 – 1:30 PM** — Lunch break (bring your own or explore nearby Shadyside restaurants)
- **1:30 – 3:30 PM** — Afternoon session: Large ensemble playing
- **3:30 PM** — Informal read-through of participant favorites

## What to Bring

- Your recorders (all sizes welcome!)
- A music stand
- A pencil
- Lunch or money for nearby restaurants

## Cost

Free for PRS members. Non-members welcome with a suggested $10 donation.

All levels are welcome. We'll have parts suitable for beginners through advanced players.
